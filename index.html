Find Out More Pay it forward!

Tell me more...

Oxford’s independent establishments are what make the city so special. These pubs, cafes and shops create the sense of community that we know and love. The spread of COVID-19 will put a huge amount of pressure on these precious local businesses; many will suffer and many will not survive. HelpYourLocal is on a mission to help local businesses to crowdfund in these tough times: to give everyone the chance to support the independents they know and love, in return for rewards and vouchers that can be redeemed after the crisis.
