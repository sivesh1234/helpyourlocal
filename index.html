Help Your High Street

COVID-19 is hurting our high streets.

We are a voluntary initiative to help community shops fight back with a cash boost.

Coronavirus threatens our community art centres, cafès, charities, pubs, restaurants, shops, and the jobs they support.

It is critical that we stay at home to save lives but our high streets will need saving too. Our local shops and the people behind them have brough character, commerce and culture (and coffee, too!) to our communities. It is now your turn to help them. Please consider buying gift cards or online products from your favourite local shops. It might just help protect jobs*.
